Bailey Host Track Club for Youth Ages 7-14

By Teresa Chipman - The Preston Citizen

Last year PHS alumi Derek Bailey began a community track program for your ages 7-14. His motivation was a junior high program in Taylorsville, Utah that he participated in when he was young. The experience helped him gain a love for track and all it offers.

"I wanted to recreate that environment in Preston," he said. "It was a big part of my growing to love track growing up. I have been impressed with the attitudes of the kids and how well many are doing, coming in with little or no experience." Bailey added long jump and high jump this year and hopes to get the shotput going as well. Though less competitive than the school program, it offers something for all levels of experience whether it be perfecting skills the youth already have or learning new ones. Bailey is excited about both the number of kids participating and the progress they are making.
